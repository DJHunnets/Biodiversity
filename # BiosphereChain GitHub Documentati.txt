## ğŸ” Core Concepts

### BI-GDP (Biosphere Intelligence-GDP)
A recalibrated growth metric that prioritizes ecosystem health, biodiversity balance, and human well-being.

### Debt-to-Nature Ratio
Global debt relative to the value of ecosystem services. Should remain below 1.5:1.

### Blockchain Parameters
Each block in BiosphereChain contains:
- Ecological investment (% of GDP)
- COâ‚‚ price per ton
- Resource extraction (% sustainable yield)
- Wealth concentration (top 1%)
- Corporate ecosystem impact score
- Timestamp & validator ID
